Delete Your ChillBill Account

Last updated: 27 May 2026

This page explains how to request deletion of your ChillBill account and the data associated with it, developed by skdev.

How to request deletion

To delete your account and associated data, send an email from the address associated with your ChillBill account to:

  1. Email rsumit123@gmail.com with the subject line "ChillBill account deletion request".
  2. Include the email address you use to sign in to ChillBill so we can locate your account.
  3. We will verify the request and delete your account, typically within 30 days.

What data is deleted

When your account is deleted, we permanently remove your personal data, including:

  • Your account profile (name, email address, profile picture)
  • Groups you created, and the expenses, splits, and settlements you recorded

What data may be kept

Where an expense or group is shared with other members, records needed to keep those other users' balances accurate may be retained in anonymized form. We may also retain limited information where required by law. Such retained data is kept no longer than necessary and is not used to identify you.

Contact

Questions about account or data deletion? Email rsumit123@gmail.com.
